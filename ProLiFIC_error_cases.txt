1. MISSING EVENT DATE
---------------------
Problem:
Some preparatory acts did not contain a valid date. The LLM assigned placeholder values such as '2017-03-00' or '1990-00-00'.

Solution:
These entries were dropped from the dataset.

Case IDs:
17G00061, 009G0101, 004G0171, 099G0293, 095G0368, 093G0572, 093G0512, 093G0359, 095G0370


2. INVALID COMMISSION NAME
--------------------------
Problem:
Commission names were incorrectly transcribed due to typographical errors.

Solution:
Commission names were manually corrected.

Case IDs:
006G0318, 010G0138
